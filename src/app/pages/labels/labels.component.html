Materials, Finishes and Durability

Emergency Turnaround Does Not Mean Compromising on Materials

Even on a same-day or next-day timeline, the label we produce has to perform in your specific application environment. We work with you to identify the right material for your job without slowing down production.

  • Label Stocks: Paper and synthetic substrates suited to your application and environment
  • Durability: Waterproof, oil-resistant and outdoor-rated materials for labels that need to hold up beyond the production floor
  • Finishes: Matte, gloss and specialty finishes depending on your brand requirements and end use
  • Adhesives: Permanent and removable options based on your surface and application needs

If you are unsure which material suits your job, tell us where the label is going and what it needs to survive. We will make the call and keep your production moving.
